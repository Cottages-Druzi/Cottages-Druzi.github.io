Location

The complex of cottages "Druzi" is located in the village of Polyanytsya, hamlet Prokhodny , 16 km from the village of Vorokhta.

Cottages are 6 km from Bukovel Resort, 30 km from Yaremche and 90 km from the nearest airport of Ivano-Frankivsk.
