Main information

Cottages "Druzi" are three two-storey twin cottages (for 8 people each) and apartments for 8 people, located in a picturesque place near the forest. About cottages:

✅On the ground floor - kitchen (electric stove, microwave, dishwasher, refrigerator, electric kettle, dishes); living room with fireplace (sofa, TV); dining room (table with chairs); bathroom (toilet, washbasin).

✅On the first floor, there are 3 double rooms (each has a double bed, a wardrobe, a TV set; two rooms have a balcony; one has a bathroom - a shower, a toilet, a washbasin, a hair dryer); bathroom (shower, toilet, washbasin, hair dryer).

✅We have equipped the kitchens in the cottages with all the necessary equipment so that you can surprise your family and friends with your culinary skills. If you do not like to think about cooking on vacation - by prior arrangement contact us and we will take care of everything.

✅Possibility to accommodate three children in a cottage without extra bed. Everything is done to make your vacation as comfortable and high quality as possible. We look forward to seeing you!
